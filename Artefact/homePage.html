What Is A Smart Home?

A smart home is one where certain items, e.g. lights, fans or blinds, are connected to the internet. They make your life more convienent as you are able to control appliances in your home remotely from your mobile phone through an internet connection. Many devices can also be controlled using voice-control technology.
